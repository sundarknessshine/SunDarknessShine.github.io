PMU

Information

🪄 Permanent Makeup (PMU) is a cosmetic procedure where special pigments are implanted into the upper layers of the skin to imitate makeup or enhance facial features — brows, lips, eyes, etc. It helps save time on daily makeup and solves aesthetic goals.

💆‍♀️ How the procedure works:

  • Consultation and selection of shape/color;
  • Disinfection and anesthesia (if needed);
  • Pigment application using a specialized machine;
  • Healing period and follow-up corrections.

📌 The full appointment usually takes 1.5–3 hours and includes discussion of the desired result and aftercare.

📌 A touch-up is recommended after 4–8 weeks.

📌 Aftercare notes:

  • Mild swelling and redness may occur during the first days.
  • Do not pick the scabs while healing.
  • Follow the aftercare guidance (protective cream, avoid water/sweat).
